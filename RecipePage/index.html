Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspons vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the metlted butter, white sugar and brown suagr until well blended.
  3. Beat in the vanilla extract and eggs until light and and creamy.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Gradually stir into the butter and sugar mixture.
  5. Drop cookie dough by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased coco sheets.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand using a wooden spoon.
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven , or until edge light browned.
  8. Cook on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to racks to cool completely.

Notes

For softer cookies, reduce baking time slightly .For crsipier cookies, increase baking time slightly. Enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies with a glass of milk!
